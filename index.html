Lucky Roll of the Dice

Welcome to "Lucky Roll of the Dice".
This is a two player game. Here are the rules: Player 1 starts by rolling the dice. The total will be added to the bottom box for each roll. If player 1 chooses, they can select "hold" which will lock in their points and give the next player a chance. First person to 100pts wins! Sounds easy right, well... if you roll doubles, you go back to zero and it is the next person's turn.
Good Luck!
